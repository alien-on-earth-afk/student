Frequently Asked Questions

Is Student Hub free to use?

Yes! Student Hub offers a free tier with access to all basic features. We also offer a premium subscription with advanced features for power users.

How do I generate MCQs from my notes?

Simply upload your notes in PDF or text format, select the key topics you want to focus on, and our AI will generate relevant multiple-choice questions tailored to your material.

Can I collaborate with other students?

Absolutely! You can create study groups, share quizzes, and even compete in group challenges to make studying more interactive and fun.

How does the leaderboard system work?

You earn points for completing quizzes, maintaining study streaks, and achieving your goals. These points determine your ranking on our global and subject-specific leaderboards.
